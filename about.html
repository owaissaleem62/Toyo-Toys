About Us Page

Toyo toys is a project of Technologies Software. It is the biggest online toy shop in Pakistan, with an extensive range of products, and the only toy shop in Pakistan catering to all age groups. Initially starting as a wholesale business, we realized we wanted to provide the same products at the same reduced price to everyone, and not just retailers. We deliver products at home on Cash on Delivery and Bank Transfer using the best Couriers in Pakistan. The mission is simple: we want to provide the best quality toys to our consumers.
